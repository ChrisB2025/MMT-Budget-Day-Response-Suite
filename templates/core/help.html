Help & FAQ

3. Share Your Responses

Campaign Tools

How do I target a social media post?
Go to the homepage and paste the URL of any post from X/Twitter, YouTube, Bluesky, Reddit, or other platforms. The system will extract the content and generate an MMT critique with shareable replies.
How do I critique a news article?
Paste the article URL in the Article Target form. For paywalled content, you can paste the text directly. You'll receive a comprehensive MMT analysis with quick responses for different platforms.
How do I file a complaint?
Enter the outlet name and describe the misinformation. The system will generate a professional complaint letter with MMT talking points. You can then email it to the outlet directly.
What are shareable replies?
For each critique, we generate ready-to-post content for X/Twitter (280 chars), Bluesky, Reddit, and article comments. All include the #mmt hashtag. Just copy and paste!

Myth Bingo

How do I generate a card?
Click "Myth Bingo" and select your difficulty level to generate a 5x5 bingo card.
How do I mark a square?
Click any square when you hear that economic myth mentioned.
How do I win?
Complete 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to achieve bingo!

Campaigns

What is a campaign?
A campaign is a coordinated effort to respond to a specific topic, outlet, or narrative. You can tag your submissions with campaign names to group related items together.
How do I view active campaigns?
Visit the Campaigns page to see all recent critiques, articles, and complaints. Use the campaign filter to view items by specific campaign tags.
