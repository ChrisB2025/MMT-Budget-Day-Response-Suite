Budget Bingo

How do I generate a card?
Click "Generate Bingo Card" and select your difficulty level.
How do I mark a square?
Click any square when you hear that myth in the budget speech.
How do I win?
Complete 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to achieve bingo!

Fact-Checking

How do I submit a claim?
Go to Fact-Check → Submit and fill in the claim, context, and severity rating.
How long does it take?
AI responses are usually generated within 30 seconds.
What is severity rating?
Rate 1-10 how misleading you think the claim is (10 = most misleading).

Rebuttals

How do I download a rebuttal?
View the rebuttal and choose Download → format (PDF, Markdown, HTML).
Can I share rebuttals?
Yes! All published rebuttals are publicly accessible and shareable.
