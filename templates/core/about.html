About MMT Campaign Suite

The MMT Campaign Suite is a year-round platform for tracking economic myths, crowdsourcing fact-checks, holding media accountable, and generating shareable MMT rebuttals for activists.

Why This Platform?

Economic myths are repeated daily across media: "taxpayer money," "maxed out credit card," "balancing the books," "government spending causes inflation." This platform provides tools to challenge these narratives with evidence-based Modern Monetary Theory analysis:

  • Target social media posts spreading economic misinformation
  • Critique news articles with comprehensive MMT analysis
  • Generate complaint letters to media outlets
  • Create shareable responses for X/Twitter, Bluesky, and Reddit
  • Track and coordinate campaigns with other activists

Campaign Tools

Social Media Target

Submit links to social media posts on X/Twitter, YouTube, Bluesky, Reddit, and more. Get AI-powered MMT critique and ready-to-post replies with the #mmt hashtag.

Article Target

Submit news articles and opinion pieces for comprehensive MMT analysis. Each critique includes:

  • Summary and key claims identified
  • Factual errors and framing issues
  • Missing context from MMT perspective
  • Quick responses for social media and letters to the editor

Complaint Generator

Draft complaints to media outlets about economic misinformation. Generate professional letters with multiple rhetorical strategies to avoid form-letter detection.

Campaigns Dashboard

View all active campaigns, filter by campaign tags, and coordinate with other activists on targeted responses.

Myth Bingo

Generate a 5x5 bingo card with common economic myths. Perfect for budget days, PMQs, or any political event. Track which myths you hear and compete with others!

Join the Community

Activists can coordinate campaigns, share targets, and workshop responses in the MMT Campaign Suite Discord workspace. Connect with other MMT advocates, get help with tricky cases, and turn individual responses into larger campaigns.

Technology

Built with Django 5, HTMX, Alpine.js, and Tailwind CSS. Powered by Claude AI (Sonnet 4.5) for intelligent analysis and content generation. All generated social media content includes the #mmt hashtag by default.
