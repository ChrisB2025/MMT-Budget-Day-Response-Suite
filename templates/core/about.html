About MMT Campaign Suite

A year-round platform for tracking economic myths, crowdsourcing fact-checks, holding media accountable, and generating shareable MMT rebuttals for activists.

Why This Platform?

Economic myths are repeated daily across media. This platform provides tools to challenge these narratives with evidence-based Modern Monetary Theory analysis.

"taxpayer money" "maxed out credit card" "balancing the books" "government spending causes inflation" "we can't afford it" "printing money"

These phrases frame economic debates in ways that misrepresent how modern monetary systems work. MMT Campaign Suite helps activists identify, critique, and respond to these myths with accurate information.
